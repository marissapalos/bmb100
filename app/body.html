1926–2026

Bulldog Marching Band Centennial Celebration

For 100 years, the marching band has been the soundtrack of the Red Wave, and the heart and soul of Fresno State. This fall, the Bulldog Marching Band and the Fresno State Band Alumni Club invite you to join in celebrating this legacy. We are offering opportunities over two weekends to reunite with classmates and relive the glory days. We hope to see you there!
