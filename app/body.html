A CENTURY · IN BRIEF

100 Years

Across five movements — the founding, the silence, the comeback, the recognition, and the homecoming we're building toward.

  1. 1926 The Band is Born
    I. ♩ Movement One — Allegro con spirito

    Thirty-six pioneers under Arthur C. Forsblad.

    The first Fresno Normal School band forms with thirty-six charter members, planting the seed of what will become a century-long tradition of Bulldog music.

    Fresno Normal School · Founding

  3. 1992 The Silent Year
    II. ♩ Movement Two — Adagio dolente

    Budget cuts silence the marching band.

    For one painful season, the program goes dark. Dr. Sutherland keeps the embers alive with a small "Spirit Band" at the games — a quieter sound, but the same heart.

    In memoriam · a season without a march

  5. 1993 The Comeback
    III. ♩ Movement Three — Vivace risoluto

    The Bulldog Marching Band returns — and the Bulldog Beat is born.

    Dr. Gilroy arrives, rebuilds the program from the ground up, and launches the Bulldog Beat alongside it. The march is on again.

    Dr. Gary P. Gilroy, Director

  7. 2017 Ensemble of Excellence
    IV. ♩ Movement Four — Maestoso

    Named one of the country's top college marching bands.

    The College Band Directors National Association names the Bulldog Marching Band among the top eight college marching bands in the United States.

    CBDNA · Top eight in the nation

  9. 2026 100 Years Strong
    V. ♩ Movement Five — Grandioso

    A century of Bulldogs. One last march together.

    One hundred years after thirty-six musicians first took the field, alumni from every era come home. Three days, one stadium, one hundred years of memory.

    CENTENNIAL · The chapter we write together
